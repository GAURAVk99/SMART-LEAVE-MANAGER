Privacy Policy for Smart Leave Manager

Last Updated: July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Smart Leave Manager ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By installing and using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

Personal Data Provided by You

To provide and maintain our role-based leave management services, we may collect or request certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Job Title
  • Department
  • Employee ID
  • Leave Request Details (dates, reasons, leave types, approvals, status)
  • Authentication and Session Settings

Automatically Collected Data

When using the App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Model
  • Operating System Version
  • Unique Device Identifiers
  • Network Connection Status
  • IP Address
  • Application Crash Reports
  • Usage Statistics

2. Third-Party Services & Advertising (Google AdMob)

To support our App, we integrate third-party services that may collect information used to identify you or personalize your experience.

Google AdMob

The App uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies, advertising identifiers, device information, and similar technologies to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

AdMob may collect:

  • Device Information
  • Advertising ID
  • IP Address
  • Approximate Location
  • Ad Interaction Data

You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting:

Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Learn more by visiting Google's Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Manage leave requests and approvals.
  • Provide secure authentication.
  • Display advertisements using Google AdMob.
  • Improve app performance and user experience.
  • Analyze application usage.
  • Provide customer support.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information.

Your information may be shared only in the following situations:

  • Within Your Organization: Authorized managers and HR administrators can access leave requests as required for leave management.
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services including cloud hosting, analytics providers, and Google AdMob.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If such information is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.
